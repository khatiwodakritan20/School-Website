Mission , Goals & Objectives

The Vision

The vision of the Sagarmatha Secondary Boarding School is to be premier  education institution at par with world-class role models.



The Mission


The Mission of Sagarmatha Secondary Boarding School is to provide quality education and help students achieve academic excellence, develop critical thinking skills, and foster a lifelong love for learning in a supportive and inclusive environment.
